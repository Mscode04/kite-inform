About App

What is Neu-Palliative App?

Neu-Palliative is a smart mobile application developed by Neuraq Technologies to streamline and digitize the daily operations of palliative care centers. Designed with both caregivers and patients in mind, the app simplifies routine tasks and ensures seamless communication, helping field nurses, volunteers, and administrators manage their responsibilities more efficiently and compassionately.

App Features

Awesome Features

Catheter Scheduling

Schedule catheter changes efficiently to ensure timely and proper care for patients without the risk of delays.

Homecare Scheduling

Manage and assign nurse home visits easily to provide continuous support and care to patients at home.

Add New Cases Easily

Quickly register new patients with minimal data entry and start providing care immediately.

Medicines Management

Maintain a digital log of all prescribed medicines and track changes or updates efficiently.

Pause/Hide Medicines

Temporarily deactivate or hide medicines from the list without deleting them to retain treatment history.

Reports

Generate various types of reports including social, equipment, and death reports with filtering options.

Progress Report (Audit)

Audit patient recovery and nurse performance with clear, real-time progress tracking and reporting tools.

Homecares

Categorize homecare visits including doctor, nurse, emergency, and volunteer types for better coordination.

Death Reports

Maintain official and detailed death reports including time, cause, and follow-up protocols.

Filters and Sort Reports

Search and filter reports by activity status, diagnosis, and timeframe like monthly or yearly.

Auto & Manual Deactivation

Automatically or manually deactivate users or entries based on predefined rules or admin actions.

Secure PIN for Deleting Records

Enable an additional layer of security by requiring a PIN before any sensitive record deletion.

Print, Download, Share Reports

Easily print, download, or share reports in a few clicks for better communication and offline access.

Easy to Search Patient

Search patients by name, ID, phone, diagnosis, or any other critical medical attribute instantly.

Search by All Details

Enable comprehensive search filters across the full patient profile and medical logs for quick data access.

Easy to Get Monthly Reports

Generate automated monthly reports categorized by patient, diagnosis, activity, or department.

Easy to Customize the App

Modify layouts, features, and access settings to suit different palliative care organizations or workflows.

Updates Option for All

Push real-time updates across all devices and user accounts to ensure everyone sees the latest information.
