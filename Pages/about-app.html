About Neuraq

Compassionate mobile solution for palliative care patients and caregivers.

Neuraq Palliative App connects volunteers, doctors, and family members with real-time updates, visit tracking, medicine reminders, and more.

Built in the heart of Makkaraparamba, the app was inspired by local community challenges in palliative care. Our goal is to bridge the gap between patients and palliative support using technology.
