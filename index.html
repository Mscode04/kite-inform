Neuraq Technologies Launches Mobile App for Palliative Care

Malappuram – Neuraq Technologies has launched a mobile application aimed at enhancing palliative care by streamlining patient management and reducing the workload of healthcare workers. The app helps doctors and nurses spend more time with patients by simplifying tracking and communication processes. The application was officially launched during the "Arike" patient gathering organized by Makkaraparamba Palliative, in the presence of Dr. P. Unni. The launch was conducted by Muhammad Musthafa Tharayil, MD of Tharayil Business Group.
